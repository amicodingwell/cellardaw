cellarDAW will be a two part project:

• A fully functional tracker style DAW
• A handheld form factor solution to operate cellarDAW from the convenience of your cellar!

The software, simply 'cellarDAW' is going to be a console-based tracker created in Rust, so users can create from the TUI or a console emulator without distraction and maximal performance.
The handheld will likely be based on a RaspberryPi 4B, PiSugar battery hat, custom buttons for operation and custom case.
